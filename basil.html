What is Computer Society of India ?

The Computer Society of India is the largest body of computer professionals in the country. Established in 1965, it has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry. CSI is a non-profit organization focused on scientific and educational advancement in Information Technology.

At Central University of Jharkhand, the CSI Student Chapter is a dynamic community where students can learn, network, and grow. We organized a National Seminar on Cybersecurity and collaborated with Google for the DevFest event. This is just the beginning there is a lot more exciting to come

Day 1: Seminar Inauguration & Paper Presentation

The seminar kicked off with the Guests and experts from computer science shared inspiring thoughts on how AI, ML, and security are connected. The keynote talk focused on why strong and reliable systems are needed in today’s world of digital threats. Students and researchers also presented papers, both online and offline, on topics like secure machine learning, data privacy, and threat detection. These highlighted the growing need for strong cybersecurity in our AI-driven world.

Day 2: Expert Insights & Accolades

The second day delved deep into practical applications with expert talks on wireless network security, lightweight cryptography, and the future of digital identity. Speakers from prestigious institutions like the University of Hyderabad and NIT Patna, along with industry CTO Mr. Gautam Hazari, bridged the gap between theory and real-world challenges. The event concluded with a grand valedictory session, celebrating the best paper presenters and reinforcing our mission to innovate.
